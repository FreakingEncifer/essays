Important points

  1. Objectivity in life
    1. Why do you want to do it?
    2. What is the end result?
    3. Whom are you pleasing?
    4. Don't be disheartened over failures
    5. Keep going. Remember why?
  2. Make right choices - say yes or no
  3. Time Rounding off problem
  4. Correct set of desires and goals
  5. Association of people around you
  6. Intense desire
  7. Pleasure starved. Whatever you give, I'll take?
  8. Meditative Happiness.

  20. Day 19: Right on time. Started Chanting at 04:01. Skipped the bath routine. Realized that I was simply enthusiastic about a few things:
    • Waking up early so that I have a lot of peaceful time.
    • Brahma muhurt is the best time to do your top most priority task. For me, it is to chant the holy names of the Lord.
    • Time for other activities in life like service, work, family time. Discipline. And at peace without guilt because you have prioritized the most important task daily.
    • Note that it never occured to me that I cared about being fresh. Keeping a Higher goal in front of you helps in your austerity to achieve it. All glories to Chanting of the Holy Names and to Srila Prabhupada to help me have the correct perspective.
    • This is not the past 3 weeks of efforts and desires. It took almost about 3 years to bring this. Hope that I don't lose it anytime. I'm grateful towards the wonderful people who inspired me.
    • Engaging in devotional service. Whatever possible. Be honest and try to help devotees in their mission.
  21. Day 20: On time. I feel that I'm enjoying this. Mainly because I can do my sadhana uncompromisingly. I'm no more worried whether I'm tired, because my focus has been shifted to what is more important than simply resting.
  22. Day 21: 30 minutes late. Still managed to complete my Chanting by 06:30

  24. Day 23: 30 mins late. Need to work on diet control. Eating heavy for dinner causes you to sleep more and causes lazy Consciousness.
  25. Day 24: 30 mins late. Got a hope that I can wake up without snoozing. Challenge is once you see the time, don't compromise on going back. Simple start your morning routine. An extra 5 mins bed turns up to be 30 mins toll which is not worth it.
