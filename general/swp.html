It is wonderful to see how when one habit is changed, it cascades into a few more bad ones and help us progress overall. Note that not all bad habits are gone, but when you are firmly situated on one good habit, it'll help you overcome others. Power naps -> sleep: Power naps are good but it is a speed breaker in your daily routine. Try to be engaged so that you procrastinate your urge to rest and take rest only in the time dedicated for it i.e. night time. Also, to give up on any gaming addiction: Remind yourself "There is no real achievement in doing this" before and after every game you play. Gradually you would lose taste in playing and realise it is a waste of time and you can invest in sublime transformations of your life.
