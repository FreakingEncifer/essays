How to solve water problem?
Maybe we can invest in building connected Canals from big rivers and intermediate dams to control the water-flow. But this control of water-flow should not be politically motivated but to be seen as a solution to avoid floods.

How to lead a good peaceful life?
Very simple. Incorporate the following simple things in your day-to-day lives:

  1. Read books written by HDG A. C. Bhaktivedanta Swami Prabhupada - Especially Bhagavad Gita As It Is
  2. Chant the
    Hare Krishna Hare Krishna Krishna Krishna Hare Hare
    Hare Rama Hare Rama Rama Rama Hare Hare
    maha-mantra and follow 4 regulative principles namely: No intoxication, No meat eating, No illicit sex and No gambling
  3. Participate in Sankirtana - loud chanting and dancing in the association of devotees
  4. Offer food to Lord Krishna and eat prashadam
  5. Spread the message.
